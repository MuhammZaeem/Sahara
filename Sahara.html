Sahara sewing machine is oldest sewing machine dealer established even before 1936. We are the brand authorised dealer for SAHARA, JACK, JUKI, BERNETTE(SWISS), BROTHER (JAPAN), DAISEN JAPAN. We deal with other brand like Singer, Usha-Janome. We supply spare parts of domestic and industrial sewing machines. Our business was renamed as Sahara Sewing Machine in 1981 as we are the only authorised dealer for Sahara machines. We are dealing with Saharmachines since 1970's. We were the authorised dealer for Pfaff, Rita, and Daisy brand sewing machines before signing our contract with Sahara. We have well equipped service centre and well experienced and trained technicians. We do our unique way of checking before we deliver.
